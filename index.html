Welcome to Alabama Animal Rescue

We are a community-centered non-profit focused on rescuing and rehoming animals throughout Alabama. Our mission is to provide safe, loving homes for dogs and cats in need while educating the public on responsible pet ownership.

Through partnerships with volunteers, foster families, and local businesses, we’re able to offer medical care, behavioral support, and adoption services to animals that might otherwise be overlooked. By visiting this site, you’re already part of that mission.

Support Our Mission

Your donations help cover food, vaccinations, spay/neuter procedures, and emergency medical care for animals in our program. Even a small monthly gift makes a big difference.

Volunteer with Us

Volunteers help with adoption events, transport, fostering, and day-to-day care. Whether you have a few hours a month or a few hours a week, there’s a way to get involved.

  • Event setup and cleanup
  • Animal socialization
  • Foster care
  • Transportation to vet appointments
