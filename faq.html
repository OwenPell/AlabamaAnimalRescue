Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about adopting, fostering, and donating.

Adoption & Donations

What is included in the adoption fee?

Adoption fees typically include spay/neuter surgery, core vaccinations, a microchip, and a basic health exam. Exact details may vary by animal, and we’ll go over them with you before finalizing an adoption.

How do I meet a specific dog or cat?

Submit a contact form or email us with the animal’s name. Our volunteers will help schedule a meet-and-greet, either at an adoption event or by appointment at our partner locations.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes. Alabama Animal Rescue is a registered non-profit organization. We can provide a receipt for any monetary or in-kind donation for your records.

Can I donate supplies instead of money?

Absolutely. We welcome donations of food, toys, cleaning supplies, and gently used beds or crates. Contact us to see our current most-needed items.

How do I become a foster?

Fill out the contact form and select “Volunteering.” We’ll send you a foster application and more information about training and support.
